A digital garden is a collection of evolving notes — not polished articles with publication dates, but living documents that grow and connect over time.

This site is my garden. Notes here range from seedlings (rough ideas) to evergreen (well-developed thoughts). They link to each other, forming a web of knowledge rather than a chronological feed.
